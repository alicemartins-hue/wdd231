Welcome to Wars & Books

We are a project that blends the fascinating worlds of history and fantasy.

Here, you'll explore books that reimagine real wars through the lens of magic, sorrow, and the depth of human experience.

War is a sensitive topic, especially in literature. It reflects a society, a group of people, and the causes they fight for. It’s not only about culture, but also about sorrow, sacrifice and love.

Our purpose is to connect readers with stories that not only inspire empathy, but also spark curiosity.

Our Motivation

For a world where wars exist only in stories

We dream of a world where the only battles fought are written in ink — where history’s pain becomes a lesson, and its courage lives on in the pages we turn.

To forget is to invite the past to return.
